Privacy Policy

Effective: [Launch Date]

We value your privacy. Beat The AI (“the Game”) collects only the minimum information required to function and improve gameplay.

What We Collect

  • Device/session ID (random UUID, not advertising ID, not linked to personal identity).
  • Gameplay events (starts/finishes, coins earned/spent, ad views).
  • Crash reports (via Sentry).

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No name, email, phone, contacts, photos, or precise location.
  • No advertising ID, no cross-app tracking.
  • No personalized ads.

Advertising

Ads are served by Google AdMob. Ads are non-personalized and do not track you across apps. AdMob may collect basic device info (e.g., OS version) to serve and measure ads.

Analytics

We use lightweight analytics (PostHog) to understand game usage. A random session ID measures play sessions, retention, and feature usage. This cannot identify you personally.

Crash Reporting

Sentry is used to diagnose crashes and errors. Reports may include device type, OS version, and stack traces. No personal information is included.

Data Sharing

Data is never sold. Shared only with the providers listed above (Google AdMob, PostHog, Sentry) to provide functionality.

Your Choices

  • You may opt out of analytics by disabling tracking in your device settings.
  • You may request data deletion by contacting us at: beat.the.ai.game@gmail.com

Children

This Game is not directed toward children under 13. Ads are rated PG or lower.
